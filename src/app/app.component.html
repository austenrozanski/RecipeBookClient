Grilled Cheese
by Austen Rozanski

Instructions
Sandwich
1. Melt butter in microwave.
2. While melting butter, head pan over stove on medium heat.
3. Brush bread slices with butter.
4. Place 2 slices of cheese between 2 slices of bread and put into pan.
5. Flip after 2 minutes or when golden brown.
6. Cook for 2 more minutes and remove from pan.
